# CS302.3
The Elite Emporium
A custom-built e-commerce platform designed specifically for Elite Beauty School, providing seamless product management, accessibility across campuses, and robust promotional control.

## About the Project
This platform is tailored to the needs of Elite Beauty School, supporting students, staff, and administration across all campuses. It centralizes product availability, streamlines promotions, and empowers staff with easy-to-use management tools. Utilising flask collaborate with Progrest SQL for data management and HTML/CSS/Bootstrap for front-end with JS and Jinja2 templating.

## Main Features
### 1. Product Browsing & Shopping
- Browse products by category (Skin, Hair, Body)  
- Filter by brand and product type  
- Add items to cart and checkout securely  
- Enter shipping details  
- Select campus at login or checkout  
### 2. Campus-Based Management
- Inventory and products managed per campus  
- Orders fulfilled by selected campus  
- Users see only products and promos from their campus  
### 3. Admin Controls
- Campus admins manage orders, stock, and campus-specific products/promos  
- Super admin oversees all campuses and global settings  
### 4. Promotions
- Campus-specific promotions and pricing  
- Limited-time offers with promo tags  
### 5. Administrative Tools
- Track activities and stock per campus  
- Restrict admin access to their campus only
